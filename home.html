Educator Panel

Tutors craft detailed profiles showcasing their educational background, teaching experience, and availability. They set subject expertise, hourly rates, manage schedules, engage in a messaging system, and track payments and feedback, creating a dynamic hub for their tutoring services.

Students/Parents Panel

Students create profiles, search for tutors based on subjects and ratings, schedule appointments, communicate through messaging, manage payments, and provide feedback. The panel serves as a personalized gateway for students to connect with qualified tutors and navigate their educational journey efficiently.
